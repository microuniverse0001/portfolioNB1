From Medical Statistics to UX/UI Aesthetics


"I'm a junior UX/UI designer. I am from Ukraine and based in Poland.

Historically, I finished my degree as a nurse and had my practice in the hospital for 3 years. I burned out because of the absence of prospects and quit. After that, I searched for a job and found one as a sales consultant. I worked for 7 months, burned out again, and starting from there, decided to move forward abroad.

I moved to Poland and tried myself in a basic physical job as a warehouse worker. Now I have 4 full years of experience in that field (8 months as a guide and leader for english speakers newcomers). During this time, I saved money to change my direction and find a new qualification that would let me grow as a unique specialist. I tried photography but failed for some reason, and then I discovered IT.

It felt like a challenge, and I took it on. I have done personal research, and it turned out that the easiest approach to the world of IT starts from design. I tried it, and here I am. I invested money and time to learn it, around 2 years, and now I am working with a mentor to guide me to the better understanding.
