About me

From Medical Statistics to UX/UI Aesthetics

My journey into the professional world began in the intricate maze of medicine, where for three transformative years, I wielded the power of numbers as a medical statistician. This role required precision and analytical prowess, skills I further honed during a brief stint as a consultant.

When I was 23, an adventurous spirit and the quest for educational resources propelled me to the beautiful landscapes of Poland. I first ventured into the realm of warehousing, a detour that lasted longer than anticipated, yet it became a backdrop for introspection and reinvention. The allure of capturing moments through a camera lens briefly swayed me, and I eagerly took to photography. However, fate had other plans when the global pandemic and geopolitical tensions shifted my trajectory once again.

I pivoted to UX/UI design, a discipline that combined my analytical skills with my burgeoning artistic sensibilities. The road wasn’t always smooth—my commitment to my dream led me back to Poland, where I showcased leadership in warehousing and resisted lucrative job offers, all in my unwavering pursuit to become a proficient UX/UI designer.

Today, after four years navigating warehouses, three years immersed in medical statistics, and several months consulting, I stand at the crossroads of logic and artistry, ever-eager to blend my diverse experiences into design solutions that resonate.
